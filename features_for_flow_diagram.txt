In homepage, user can 
1.Manage their profile
2.Search for users 
3.Follow their favourite artists
4.Know the upcoming auction(Timer is on home page)
5.Upload their painting for auction sale
6.In live auction page, they can see the live auction and can bid for auction
7.In upcoming auction page, They can see the details of upcoming auctions
8.In auction results page, They can see the all sold and passed paintings with bidding details
9.In buy page, They can buy paintings uploaded by admin without bidding(for original rate)
10.After winning they receive an email for payment and can do payment process further(payment simulation)
11.In the footer they can see the contact details of the portal
